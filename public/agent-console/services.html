How Knowledge Containers Work
One card per topic — Groq answers any caller question from it

Each Knowledge Container is a rich briefing document for one topic — a service, special, policy, or FAQ. Add as many labelled sections as you need: Price, What's Included, Warranty, Duration, Availability, etc. Write as much as you want.

When a caller asks about that topic, Groq reads the full container and synthesises a natural spoken answer within your configured word limit. It then naturally offers to book — or appends your exact fixed phrase — depending on your settings. The caller never hears "I don't know" again.
