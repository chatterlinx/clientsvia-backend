Purpose

This SMS notification system is used exclusively for internal platform health monitoring and critical system alerts sent to our technical operations team.

Who Receives These Messages?

  • Development team members - responsible for platform maintenance
  • System administrators - managing infrastructure and databases
  • On-call engineers - responding to critical incidents

Total Recipients: 2-5 internal staff members
Message Volume: Low (estimated 10-50 messages per day)

Message Types

Recipients receive operational alerts including:

  • Platform health check results
  • Critical system error notifications
  • Database connectivity warnings
  • API outage alerts
  • Redis cache failures
  • Twilio service disruptions

Opt-In Process

üîí Authentication Required

Staff members configure alert preferences through our secure, authentication-required admin dashboard. The opt-in process is:

  1. Staff member logs in to admin platform with credentials
  2. Navigates to Notification Center ‚Üí Settings tab
  3. Manually enters their contact information (name, phone, email)
  4. Selects alert preferences (SMS, Email, Calls checkboxes)
  5. Clicks "Add Contact" to explicitly consent

This is Business-to-Employee (B2E) messaging. All recipients are authorized employees who have opted in as part of their job responsibilities.

Opt-Out Process

Staff members can opt out at any time by:

  • Removing their contact information through the admin dashboard Settings tab, OR
  • Replying STOP to any alert message

Compliance & Privacy

No Customer Data: This system does NOT send messages to customers, clients, or the general public. Recipients are internal employees only.

TCPA Compliance: As B2E messaging with explicit employment-based consent, this system is exempt from consumer TCPA requirements.

Data Security: All contact information is stored securely and accessible only to authenticated administrators.
